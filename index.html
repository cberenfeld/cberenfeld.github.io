Clément Berenfeld

Universität Potsdam

Briefly

I am a postdoc at the Institut für Mathematik of Uni-Potsdam, under the supervision of Alexandra Carpentier and Nicolas Verzelen.
Before that, I did my PhD at CEREMADE, Université Paris-Dauphine under the supervision of Marc Hoffmann. You can find my PhD manuscript here.
My research lies in mathematical statistics with a focus on problems arising from geometric inference and random graphs.
Here is a short CV (June 2024).
