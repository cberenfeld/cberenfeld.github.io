Clément Berenfeld

PhD. Candidate at Paris-Dauphine University (started in 2019).
Under the supervision of Prs. Marc Hoffmann and Jacques Féjoz, CEREMADE.

Research interests

  • Statistical estimation problems on manifold-supported probability measures;
  • Minimax theory, model selection and adaptive estimation;
  • Inference on dynamical systems : recovering information from noisy observations of dynamical systems solutions, arising from celestial mechanics, biology, computational chemistry...
